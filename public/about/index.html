Problem & Solution

Current products require monthly data plan through mobile service provider. Our solution is no recurring fees plus LoRaWAN technology for zero-cost data transmissions.

Features

Tracking your pet with alerts sent directly to your phone. Custom geofence capabilities allowing user to set a virtual fence.

Components

Gateway
Routes LoRa packets to The Things Network. Simple setup steps that takes less than 5 minutes.
Sparkfun Pro RF
24 hour battery life & rechargeable in under 4 hours.
Sparkfun GPS
Relays GPS coordinates to Pro RF module. Ensures canine companion is safe and secure within specified geofence.
